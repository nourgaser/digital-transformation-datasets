Dar Al Maaref is an Egyptian company that operates in the field of publishing and printing. It creates various types of literature, such as educational materials and novels. Thus, its products can be categorized as books, educational books, or academic books. Dar Al Maaref operates through a hybrid business model, selling directly to consumers and providing schools, libraries, and distributors with educational literature.


Power BI Dashboard can assist the managers of Dar Al Maaref in decision-making in different areas of business operations. For instance, managers will be able to track sales performance, make informed decisions regarding reprinting books, estimate efficiency of inventory management, etc.



In order to facilitate effective decision-making, the Power BI dashboard must display several KPIs. Among others, KPIs of financial performance revenue, profit margin, and other financial figures , sales KPIs, inventory KPIs, production KPIs, and KPIs related to human resources can help the management better understand current operations of the company and plan its future development. The key performance indicators related to sales include sales by category, best-selling books, and orders made. The key performance indicators associated with operations include inventory levels, inventory turnover, and order processing time. Lastly, the key performance indicators concerning customers include the number of customers, retention rate, and sales by geographical region.
