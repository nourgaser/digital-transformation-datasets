1. Brief Description of the Company 

Misr Insurance Company operates in the insurance industry, specifically in non-life (general) insurance. 

Business Model 

The company follows a service-based business model, where it generates revenue by: 

Selling insurance policies to individuals and businesses 

Collecting premiums 

Managing risk and paying claims when insured events occur 

Key Products / Services 

Property insurance 

Motor (car) insurance 

Marine and cargo insurance 

Travel insurance 

Engineering and industrial insurance 

Corporate risk coverage 

The company serves both individual customers and large organizations, making it one of the leading insurance providers in Egypt.

Which insurance products generate the highest profit? 

Which regions/customers have the highest claims risk? 

How to improve customer retention and acquisition? 

How to optimize pricing and reduce losses? 

How to improve operational efficiency in claims processing?

Customer KPIs
